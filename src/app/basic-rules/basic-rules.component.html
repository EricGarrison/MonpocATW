Basic Rules

Applies to all alliances
If you're new to Monsterpocalypse (or want a simplified version of this game mode), then start here. All perks are the same for all alliances. All perks are replaced when using advanced alliance perks.
All units and monsters in your force must be from the same alliance.
You gain certain abilities based on your alliance's control.
Note: Advanced mode has been better tested for balance.

Global Perk

Once per turn, when you spawn a unit, reduce its cost by 1.

Territory Perks

[Tier 1]

Addrenaline

+1 blue to all attacks.
Be it a need to defend, a need to take, or simple bloodlust; the result of recent battles have caused your units to fight harder.

[Tier 2]

Title incoming

+1 speed for all allied models.
Whether pursuing a fleeing enemy, or fleeing from the enemy; your troops find themselves motivated to move just a little bit faster.

[Tier 3]

Fortifications

+1 defense to all allied models.
Whether it be having the resources to dig in or carry out a siege, your forces know they can take their time and be careful. This care allows them to better protect themselves against the onslaught of your foes.

Desperation Perks

Desperate Stand

+1 blue to all attacks. +1 speed for all allied models. +1 defense to all allied models.
Nothing fights hardered than something cornered, and your forces know their backs are against the proverbial wall.
